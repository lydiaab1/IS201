Our Journey

    • We began our adventure by planning out or schedule to figure out where to go and when we would go. Everyone bought food for lunches and dinner and combines them together to make healthy meals to share. We wanted to make sure we had enough water for the trip and knew there would be fresh water along the way so we brought refillable water bottles with us.


    • Our journey began with a little prayer thanking God for this beautiful world. We had a plan to go into the forest to camp and learn more about the environment around us. Spending multiple days out in the wild is something that people don't do very often because we have shelter and food in more accessible areas


    • As we hiked there were some difficulties that we had to plan around and figure out how we were going to get past them. In the end we had an amazing time, we saw beautiful stars and plants and animals and we learned a lot about each other.
